VX Software Upgrade Plan
The Software Upgrade Plan (SUP) license works in units of "channel-years". One channel-year represents the use of one licensed channel for one year.

Commissioning or decommissioning channels will trigger a re-calculation of the SUP expiration date to project how long the active channels will take to use the remaining channel-years on the license.

There may be a 72-hour holding period for decommisioning channels.
